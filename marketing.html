wach: Movie & Show Watchlist

Your simple media library.

Designed for cinephiles and casual viewers alike, wach is a modern and minimalist watchlist app that makes it easy to manage all the movies and TV shows you’ve watched and want to watch.

Stop getting lost in the sea of content. With wach, you can build your own personal media library, discover new titles, and decide what to watch next in seconds.

Key Features:

  • Simple & Powerful Lists: Effortlessly manage what you’ve Watched and what’s on your Watchlist in two clean, separate tabs.
  • Quick Search: Instantly find any movie or show from our vast database.
  • Detailed Information: Access all the important details in one place, including IMDb ratings, plot summaries, cast, director, and release year.
  • Discover New Titles: Explore popular and curated lists to find your next favorite movie or show.
  • Sort & Filter: Organize your lists by date added, title, release year, or IMDb rating to always find what you're looking for.
  • Data Management & Privacy: Export your entire library as a JSON file for backup and import it anytime. Your privacy is a priority—all of your data is stored locally and only on your device.
  • Modern Interface: Enjoy a clean, fluid, and beautiful interface designed for a modern iOS experience.
